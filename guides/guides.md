# Guides

Explore our collection of guides to learn how Gibbon can be installed for testing and how you can contribute to its development.

Can't find the guide you're looking for? Open an issue on the [GibbonEdu/docs](https://github.com/GibbonEdu/docs/issues) repository to let us know.
